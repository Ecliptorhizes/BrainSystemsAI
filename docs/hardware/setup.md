# Hardware Setup

## EEG Devices

### OpenBCI
- Open-source, hackable
- Multiple headset options (Ultracortex, Cyton, Ganglion)
- Good for learning and customization

### Emotiv
- Consumer-grade, easier setup
- Epoc, Epoc+ or Insight
- Limited channels but sufficient for basic motor imagery

### Research-Grade
- Biosemi, g.tec, Brain Products
- Higher cost, more channels, better signal quality

## Electrode Placement

For motor imagery, focus on **sensorimotor cortex** (C3, C4, Cz and neighbors in 10–20 system).

- **Left hand imagery** → right hemisphere (C4)
- **Right hand imagery** → left hemisphere (C3)
- **Feet imagery** → Cz

## Safety

- Use gel/conductive paste as specified by the device
- Do not exceed recommended current levels
- Clean electrodes after use
- Follow manufacturer guidelines

## Robot Options

- **Arduino + servos:** Simple, low cost
- **ROS-based robots:** TurtleBot, Fetch, custom arms
- **Serial/HTTP APIs:** Many robots expose simple control interfaces
